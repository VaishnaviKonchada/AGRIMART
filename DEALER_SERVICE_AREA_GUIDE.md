# 📍 Dealer Service Area Management - Quick Start Guide

## What is This Feature?

The **Service Area Management** feature allows you (as a transport dealer) to specify exactly which areas you can provide pickup and drop-off services. This helps match your services with customer requests in your operational zone.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Access Service Areas
- Go to your **Dealer Dashboard**
- Look for **📍 Service Areas** button in the Quick Actions section
- Click it to open the Service Area Manager

### 2. Select Your Service Areas

**For Pickup Locations** (where customers pick up items):
1. Open the **District Dropdown** at the top
2. Select a district (e.g., "Visakhapatnam")
3. Open the **Mandal Dropdown** 
4. Select a mandal (e.g., "Visakhapatnam" or "Anakapalle")
5. Click **➕ Add as Pickup Location**
6. The mandal appears as a blue chip

**For Drop Locations** (where customers drop off items):
1. Select another district (or same district)
2. Select a mandal
3. Click **➕ Add as Drop Location**
4. The mandal appears as a yellow chip

### 3. Manage Your Locations

**To Remove a Location**:
- Click the **✕** button on any location chip
- Location is immediately removed from the list

**To Search Locations**:
- Use the search boxes under "Pickup Locations" or "Drop Locations"
- Type mandal name to filter the list

### 4. Save Your Changes
- Review your selections:
  - Blue chips = Pickup locations
  - Yellow chips = Drop locations
- Click the **💾 Save** button
- Wait for the success message
- Locations are now saved to your profile

### 5. View Your Statistics
- After adding locations, you'll see stats showing:
  - **Pickup Locations**: Number of pickup areas
  - **Drop Locations**: Number of drop areas
  - **Total Routes**: Possible pickup-drop combinations

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many locations can I add?**  
A: Unlimited! Add as many as you can service.

**Q: Can I change locations later?**  
A: Yes! You can add/remove locations anytime.

**Q: Will customers see my service areas?**  
A: Yes! They'll only see pickup/drop options that match your configured areas.

**Q: What if I want to expand?**  
A: Go back to Service Areas, add new locations, and save!

**Q: Do I need different vehicles for different routes?**  
A: Not required, but it helps. You can manage vehicles in "My Vehicles" section.

**Q: How is pricing calculated?**  
A: Based on distance and vehicle type. Service areas just help match requests to you.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: "Mandals not showing"
- Solution: Select a district first, then choose mandal

**Problem**: "Cannot find my location"
- Solution: Search using the search box
- Check spelling of mandal name

**Problem**: "Save button not working"
- Solution: Add at least one location
- Check your internet connection

**Problem**: "Changes not saving"
- Solution: Wait for success message
- Check for error notifications
- Try again after page refresh
